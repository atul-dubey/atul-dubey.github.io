IoT Applications in Industries

Event Brief

School students are the future of the country. They are going to be the next leaders. It becomes our responsibilty to ensure they are directed in the right path.
Technology is going to transform the world no matter whether we become part of it or not. It better to lead the tide rather that getting swept away with it. Ensuring the young generation is always on the edge of the technology has become more important than ever.
This event was to give a breif overview of how IoT technology is applied to industrial applications, and how should we proceed with the problem and solve it in the best way possible.
